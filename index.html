Introduction

I am a Virtual Medical Assistant (VMA) providing remote support to healthcare providers. I handle administrative tasks, patient communication, telehealth coordination, and record management to ensure smooth clinical operations.

Tools I Use

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) systems
  • Telehealth platforms: Zoom, Microsoft Teams
  • Scheduling and appointment software
  • Email and secure messaging tools

What I Offer

As a dedicated Virtual Medical Assistant, I provide compassionate and efficient support to healthcare professionals and patients alike. From managing appointments and medical records to assisting with patient communication and telehealth session coordination, my goal is to streamline healthcare delivery and enhance patient experience remotely.

Skills I Have Acquired

  1. Medical Documentation:Accurate and confidential handling of patient records, history, and telehealth notes.

  2. Appointment Management:Scheduling and coordinating virtual consultations efficiently across multiple platforms.

  3. Patient Communication:Providing empathetic and clear communication to patients regarding appointments and follow-ups.

  4. Telehealth Technology Proficiency:Skilled with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and telemedicine platforms for smooth virtual sessions.

  5. Administrative Support:Handling billing inquiries, insurance verification, and basic healthcare customer service remotely.
